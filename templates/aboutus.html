Our App

When trading equities, it is common to perform three types of analysis: technical, fundamental, and sentiment. Our goal is to help simplify this process. Our approach? Serving interactive analytics as a dashboard, thereby allowing the user to see the following in real time:

  • Closing price for a user-selected stock (100 M5 data points), plotted along with trading volume and a 20-period moving average,
  • Recent news headlines for the user-selected stock, and
  • Current macroeconomic events.

For more information, please review our scope of work.

Our Suggested Usage

Select a stock ticker symbol from the dropdown menu and peruse at your leisure the price action, stock news, and economic calendar--it's as easy as that!
